Destinations

Agenon-901

Nestled among the stars in a distant corner of the far galaxy is the lush, verdant planet of Agenon-901. It is a planet of rolling green hills and towering mountain ranges, all teeming with a diverse array of plant life unlike anything Earth exhibits.

Dramangode

This is a moon home to a current mining operation of NASA of valuable ore, pyrostilanium, which is currently being experimented on for fascinating uses.

Bizzario

Located in the far reaches of the observable universe, Bizarrio hosts a wondrous display of white aura lights surrounding its atmosphere, making for a phenomenal light show.

Terran-57

Terran-57 is a large aquatic planet, with the surface consisting almost entirely of water. The strange orbital patterns of the planet cause enormous waves, whirlpools, tornadoes, and much more to form on the surface, a truly wondrous sight to see.

Vanichor

Vanichor is a barren, desolate planet located in the outer reaches of the galaxy. It is covered in vast deserts of red sand and rocky cliffs, with no vegetation or animal life to be found. The planet's surface is scarred with the remains of ancient civilizations and ruins, hinting at a history of habitation long since abandoned.

Rooming

After booking your flight, you will be provided with your own room complete with all your necessities and more! Each room is provided with a bed or two, a mini-kitchen, large bathroom, and a pullout couch. 24/7 service is available to all rooms. When booking your flight, please make sure to note any special preferences or medical conditions so that we may accommodate for your needs!
